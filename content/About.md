After years of owning the domain, I finally decided to use it and return to putting some content out there.

This one is about Neovim, my current daily editor for everything.

<!-- more -->

I don't see myself as an inspirational leader[^1] 😂 but I like the thought model of "why, how, what", so here we go:

## Why

Because I felt the itch to write about topics of my interest.

I have no other intention than the process of doing it. I have no aspirations to help anyone, show off or to be correct with my content. It's up to every consumer of this content to use their brain while consuming.

Intentions might change as early as _tomorrow_.

## How

By casually pu(bli)shing, when I find the time and motivation.

I try to not recommend things, but tell about my experiences and perspectives, so anyone can draw their own conclusions.

I just started with GitHub Pages, Zola and liked the radion theme for its features. Though I might be playing around with themes, hence paths might change.

## What

Information, anecdotes, opinion pieces and resources about our favorite editor Neovim - as you might guess by the domain.

## Who

But, who am I even?

I'm your average European xennial[^2], grown up in a peaceful village with a caring family. I have always been a sucker for tech, became a professional iOS developer in 2012, came to be a Tech Lead iOS and am now monitoring and exploring the hot topic "AI", so we don't fall for the hype bullshit, but don't miss out on the important bits either.

### But it's Dr. Jekyll!

It's a wordplay. All my aliases contain "Jack". The one for tech and development related topics is "DrJackyl".
